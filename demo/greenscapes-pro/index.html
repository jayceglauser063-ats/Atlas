About Us

Austin's Premier Landscaping Team

GreenScapes Pro was founded in 2014 with a simple mission: transform ordinary yards into extraordinary outdoor living spaces. Over the past decade, we've grown into one of Austin's most trusted landscaping companies, serving over 500 residential and commercial properties.

Our team of certified landscape designers, horticulturists, and skilled craftsmen brings together years of expertise and a genuine passion for creating beautiful, sustainable outdoor environments. We use eco-friendly practices and premium materials in every project.
